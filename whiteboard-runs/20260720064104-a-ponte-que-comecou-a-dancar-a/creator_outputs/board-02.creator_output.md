### Visual Concept: Aeroelastic Flutter & Solutions

- **Style**: Hand-drawn engineer's notebook sketch on a warm parchment background (`#faf8f3`).
- **Color Palette**: Charcoal lines (`#1a2332`), Ocean-blue annotations (`#2d5a7b`), Soft green accent.

#### Layout & Elements:

1. **Left Section (The Physics)**:
   - A cyclic diagram illustrating "Aeroelastic Flutter".
   - Wind force vectors interacting with a twisting bridge deck, showing a feedback loop with circular arrows.
2. **Center Section (The Testing)**:
   - A modern wind tunnel testing chamber.
   - A scale model of a bridge deck with smooth, aerodynamic smoke lines flowing cleanly over and under it.
3. **Right Section (The Solution)**:
   - An engineering clipboard displaying a detailed bridge blueprint and mathematical formulas.
   - A large, soft green checkmark of approval stamped over the design.
