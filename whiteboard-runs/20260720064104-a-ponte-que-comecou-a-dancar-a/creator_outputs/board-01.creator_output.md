### Visual Concept: The Tacoma Narrows Mystery

- **Style**: Hand-drawn engineer's notebook sketch on a warm parchment background (`#faf8f3`).
- **Color Palette**: Charcoal lines (`#1a2332`), Ocean-blue annotations (`#2d5a7b`), Soft red accent.

#### Layout & Elements:

1. **Left Section (The Phenomenon)**:
   - A giant steel suspension bridge twisting and undulating like a flexible ribbon.
   - A large, hand-drawn ocean-blue question mark hovering above it.
2. **Center Section (The Myth vs. Reality)**:
   - A giant tornado/hurricane symbol crossed out with a soft red "X" (indicating it wasn't just a simple storm).
   - Gentle, dashed wind lines pushing against the bridge deck to show steady, moderate wind.
3. **Right Section (The Famous Victim)**:
   - A classic 1940s car bouncing precariously on a wavy segment of the bridge deck.
   - Hand-written label in ocean-blue: _"Gertie Galopante"_ (Galloping Gertie).
