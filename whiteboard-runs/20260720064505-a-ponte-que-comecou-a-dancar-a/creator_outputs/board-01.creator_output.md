# Board 01: The Dancing Bridge (Tacoma Narrows)

### Visual Style

- **Type**: Hand-drawn engineer's notebook sketch / Continuous line art
- **Background**: Parchment texture (`#faf8f3`)
- **Colors**: Charcoal (`#1a2332`) for main structures, Ocean-Blue (`#2d5a7b`) for annotations and wind vectors

### Layout & Elements

- **Left Section**: A rigid, structured steel bridge that smoothly morphs into a fluid, wavy ribbon-like structure.
- **Center Section**: Minimalist wind vector arrows pointing towards the bridge, illustrating energy transfer.
- **Right Section**: A simplified, cute 1940s vintage car bouncing on the undulating bridge deck.
- **Text Annotation**: Handwritten label near the car reading _"Gertie Galopante"_.
