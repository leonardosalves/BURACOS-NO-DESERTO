### Board 01: Aeroelastic Flutter & Instability

**Visual Style & Aesthetic:**

- **Style:** Continuous line art, engineer's notebook sketch, whiteboard explanation.
- **Color Palette:** Parchment background (`#faf8f3`), charcoal lines (`#1a2332`), ocean-blue annotations (`#2d5a7b`), soft green highlights.

**Layout & Composition:**

- **Left Section:** A storm cloud marked with a red 'X' alongside a gentle breeze blowing leaves, symbolizing wind forces.
- **Center Section:** A classic 1940s car bouncing on an undulating, wavy bridge deck (highlighted in soft green) to illustrate physical instability.
- **Right Section:** Blue aerodynamic wind arrows forming vortexes around a bridge cross-section, labeled "Flutter Aeroelástico".
- **Overall:** Minimalist, clean, and educational layout.
